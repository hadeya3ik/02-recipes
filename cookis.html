Ingredients

  • soft butter (1 cup)
  • brown sugar (1 cup)
  • white sugar (3/4 cup)
  • 2 eggs
  • flour (2 + 1/3 cup)
  • baking soda (3/4 tsp)
  • salt (1 tsp)
  • choc chips (1 + 1/2 cup)

Steps

  1. whip butter and both sugars for 3 minutes
  2. mix eggs one at a time until combined
  3. pour in flour as well as salt and baking soda and kneed gently
  4. combine chocolate chips
  5. shape into balls and place on parchment paper
  6. bake at 325 for 8 minutes, then flip the tray and bake for additional 4
